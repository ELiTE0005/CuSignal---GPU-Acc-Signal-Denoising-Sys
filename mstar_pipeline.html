PIPELINE CONTEXT

Every MSTAR chip contains two zones: the target (bright scattering center) and the surrounding clutter background (ground return, shadows, speckle noise). The GA evolves filter weights by maximising TCR — the ratio of target energy to background energy. Fitness = Pd − Pfa, evaluated per generation.
